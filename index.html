favorite books

Jane Eyre is my favorite book because it illustrates the life of a young girl, and describes the way she grows up. Time after time, she is presented with decisions that truly would only affect her on a personal level for she is accountable to no one but herself. These decisions raise a lot of moral issues, an Jane manages to keep her morality intact. I've always looked up to the way that Jane stuck to what she believed and what was right, even when no one else was watching her. My other two favorite books are The Count of Monte Cristo, and Les Miserables. However, the Harry Potter books and Narnia books are up there as well.

favorite meals

Hochstatter's Pizza: The greasiest, meatiest woodfired pizza on the planet, crafted at Chico's Pizza in Moses Lake, WA. The crispy thin crust is piled high with pepperoni, Canadian bacon, regular bacon, olives, cheese and sauce. Recommended sides include french fries, rootbeer, and your choice of antacid.

Traditional Thanksgiving Dinner: Yep, I'm one of those people. I could eat the full Thanksgiving dinner multiple times in a year. I love turkey with mashed potatoes and gravy, stuffing, and all the rest. The one thing I'll leave out is cranberry sauce.

Ice Cream: I am a firm believer that a good ice cream cone or milkshake counts as a meal. The cone gives you a serving of grain, while the ice cream itself (if you choose well) gives you a serving of fruit, dairy, and your daily sugars and fats. My favorite is from Sandpoint Cone and Coffee.
